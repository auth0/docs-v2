To help clients defend against mix-up attacks, enable the **Include Issuer in Authorization Responses** toggle. When enabled, Auth0 includes the `iss` parameter identifying the authorization server in authorization responses.

1. Navigate to **Settings** on the left sidebar of the [Auth0 Dashboard](https://manage.auth0.com/dashboard/).
2. Select [**Advanced**](https://manage.auth0.com/dashboard/#/tenant/advanced) in the top right corner.
3. Scroll down to the **Settings** section and enable the **Include Issuer in Authorization Responses** toggle.
