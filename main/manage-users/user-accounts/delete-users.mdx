1. Go to the [Dashboard > User Management > Users](https://manage.auth0.com/#/users).
2. Click the name of the user you want to delete.
3. On the **Details** tab, scroll to the bottom, next to **Delete user**, and click **Delete**.<br />

   A popup will warn you that the action cannot be undone and prompt you to confirm that you want to delete the user. Click the **Yes, Delete It** button to confirm. This will permanently delete the user.
