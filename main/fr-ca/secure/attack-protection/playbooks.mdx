Use the following playbooks to help protect your users and tenant from attacks.

### Bot detection playbook

The [bot detection playbook](/docs/fr-ca/secure/attack-protection/playbooks/bot-detection-playbook) helps you identify bots trying to log into your tenant.

### Breached password playbook

The [breached password playbook](/docs/fr-ca/secure/attack-protection/playbooks/breached-password-playbook) helps you identify and investigate potential attacks and take the required remediation actions.

### Brute force protection playbook

The [brute force protection playbook](/docs/fr-ca/secure/attack-protection/playbooks/brute-force-protection-playbook) describes common brute-force attack techniques and provides guidance on how to identify and investigate potential attacks against your tenant.

### MFA playbook

The [MFA playbook](/docs/fr-ca/secure/attack-protection/playbooks/mfa-playbook) describes common <Tooltip href="/docs/fr-ca/glossary?term=multifactor-authentication" tip="Authentification multifacteur (MFA)
Processus d’authentification de l’utilisateur qui utilise un facteur en plus du nom d’utilisateur et du mot de passe, tel qu’un code par SMS." cta="Voir le glossaire">MFA</Tooltip> attack vectors and provides guidance on how to investigate them.
