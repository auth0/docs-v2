---
title: "Auth0's Unified Phone Experience"
'description': "Learn about Auth0's Unified Phone Experience for phone provider configuration."
---

Auth0 allows you to configure a phone provider for <Tooltip data-tooltip-id="react-containers-DefinitionTooltip-0" href="/docs/ja-jp/glossary?term=multifactor-authentication" tip="多要素認証（MFA）: ユーザー名とパスワードに加えて、SMS経由のコードなどの要素を使用するユーザー認証プロセス。" cta="用語集の表示">Multi-Factor Authentication</Tooltip> (MFA) and <Tooltip data-tooltip-id="react-containers-DefinitionTooltip-0" href="/docs/ja-jp/glossary?term=passwordless" tip="パスワードレス: 最初の要素としてパスワードに依存しない認証の形式。" cta="用語集の表示">Passwordless</Tooltip> at the tenant-level with the Unified Phone Experience. This eliminates the need to configure a phone provider in multiple locations while maintaining flexibility when configuring delivery methods.

Tenants with configured phone providers can migrate to the Unified Phone Experience and maintain phone messaging capabilities in one place. New tenants are automatically enrolled in the Unified Phone Experience.
