Use the following playbooks to help protect your users and tenant from attacks.

### Bot detection playbook

The [bot detection playbook](/docs/ja-jp/secure/attack-protection/playbooks/bot-detection-playbook) helps you identify bots trying to log into your tenant.

### Breached password playbook

The [breached password playbook](/docs/ja-jp/secure/attack-protection/playbooks/breached-password-playbook) helps you identify and investigate potential attacks and take the required remediation actions.

### Brute force protection playbook

The [brute force protection playbook](/docs/ja-jp/secure/attack-protection/playbooks/brute-force-protection-playbook) describes common brute-force attack techniques and provides guidance on how to identify and investigate potential attacks against your tenant.
