Reduce implementation time with Auth0-reviewed integrations that you can trust. The Auth0 platform is inherently extensible, allowing you to meet your specific needs by tailoring identity flows with custom code and integrating with third-party applications and tools.

Go to [Auth0 Marketplace](https://marketplace.auth0.com/) to find and enable third-party identity solutions that integrate with our products.

<Callout icon="file-lines" color="#0EA5E9" iconType="regular">

By installing a third-party Marketplace integration with Auth0 Manage Dashboard and Management API, you agree to the integration's Terms of Use and Privacy Policy, and you accept and agree to Auth0's Marketplace [Terms of Use](https://auth0.com/legal).

</Callout>
