You can change your application's enabled connections using the <Tooltip tip="Auth0 Dashboard: Auth0's main product to configure your services." cta="View Glossary" href="/docs/glossary?term=Auth0+Dashboard">Auth0 Dashboard</Tooltip>.

1. Go to [Dashboard > Applications](https://manage.auth0.com/#/applications) and click the name of the application to view.

   <Frame>![Dashboard Applications List](/images/cdy7uua7fh8z/1ecNwGgFQZxdP57p0tp3jT/cd608fcfae22e195b604e2707e5a848d/App_List_-_EN.png)</Frame>
2. Click the **Connections** tab, and enable or disable the appropriate connections for the application.
