---
description: Filter Auth0 logs by event type to surface errors, warnings, and successful operations across your tenant for monitoring and incident response.
title: Filter Log Events
---
You can filter logs for errors, warnings, and success events in the [Dashboard](https://manage.auth0.com/#/logs) when you click the **Filter** down arrow.
